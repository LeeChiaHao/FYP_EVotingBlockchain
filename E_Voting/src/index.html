Welcome,

This is an E-Voting based Blockchain system.

By using our system, we provide the user:

  1. Authenticity

  3. Accuracy
  4. Verifiability

When using our system, please ensure your Metamask account is always connected.

Here is the brief intro of our functions:

  • To view all the available elections and cast a vote, click Cast a Vote.
  • To view the end elections' result, click Election Result.
  • To view your vote history and verify, click Vote History.
  • To view or edit your profile, click Profile.
